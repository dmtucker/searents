Living at Urbana

Urbana Apartments represents the best that Seattle has to offer. Centrally located and featuring the most amazing amenities, this LEED certified community offers an unrivaled urban living experience. You can grab a bite to eat and enjoy some shopping just beyond your doorstep. Urbana’s impressive rooftop sundeck is the perfect place for residents to socialize while enjoying the city views and the warmth of the fireplace. Inside, gorgeous finishes and spacious floorplans invoke all the comforts of home.

Ballard

Urbana is a LEED Gold Certified community in Seattle's Ballard neighborhood. What was once a blue-collar fishing and boat-making community has turned into one of Seattle's hippest hotspots, full of eclectic shops and eateries along the city's historic waterfront. Whether you want to live near the bay or are looking for a walkable neighborhood with convenience at every turn, Ballard has it all. Check out the map and see what makes our area so special and plan your visit today!
